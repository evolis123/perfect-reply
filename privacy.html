Privacy Policy

Your privacy is critically important to us.

We do not store your emails. When you paste an email and your reply goal into our form, that information is sent directly to the Google AI API to generate a response. We do not save, log, or store any part of your email content on our servers after the reply is generated.

We do not use your data for training. The content of your emails is not used to train our AI models or for any other purpose.

Our goal is to be a trustworthy tool. Your data is your own, and we are committed to keeping it that way.
